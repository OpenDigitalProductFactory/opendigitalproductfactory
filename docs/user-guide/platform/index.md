## Overview

Platform is the administration surface for capabilities that support the whole product: AI workforce configuration, tool services, integrations, identity, audit, and operational platform settings.

## Key Concepts

- **AI Workforce** — Provider configuration, model routing, agent assignments, skills, and authority controls.
- **Tools and services** — Approved tools, MCP services, integrations, and service activation workflows.
- **Identity and authorization** — Internal users, groups, agents, applications, and permission boundaries.
- **Audit and authority** — Logs that show which users, agents, and tools took action.

## What You Can Do

- Configure AI providers and routing policies
- Review available platform tools and integrations
- Manage identity and authorization surfaces
- Inspect audit trails for agent and user activity
- Keep platform services aligned with governance and release controls
