## Use This Doc For

- `/compliance/submissions`

## Workflow

1. Confirm the submission obligation and due window.
2. Assemble the required evidence and source data.
3. Record the submission state clearly so follow-up is auditable.

## What To Watch

- submissions prepared without confirmed source data
- due dates tracked outside the submission record
- post-submission follow-up not being captured
