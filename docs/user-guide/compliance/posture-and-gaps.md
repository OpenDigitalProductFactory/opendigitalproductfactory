## Workflow

1. Read posture as the current summary, not the source record.
2. Open the underlying gap when posture says something needs work.
3. Convert unresolved gaps into owned remediation work.

## What To Watch

- posture dashboards being treated as proof by themselves
- long-lived gaps without corrective ownership
- summary scores masking specific weak areas
