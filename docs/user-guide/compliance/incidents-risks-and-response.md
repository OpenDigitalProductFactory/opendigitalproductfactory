## Workflow

1. Record the incident or risk clearly.
2. Separate immediate response from longer-term remediation.
3. Tie resulting actions back into the relevant compliance workstream.

## What To Watch

- incidents being logged without containment or next-step clarity
- risks staying abstract instead of driving concrete mitigation
- response work happening outside the compliance record
