## Workflow

1. Review the audit scope and findings.
2. Translate findings into corrective actions with owners and due dates.
3. Close the action only when evidence supports the fix.

## What To Watch

- audit findings closed without linked corrective evidence
- corrective actions that do not clearly map back to the audit issue
- repeated audit themes that never feed a broader control improvement
