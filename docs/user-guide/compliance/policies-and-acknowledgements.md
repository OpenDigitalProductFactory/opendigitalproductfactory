## Workflow

1. Review the policy intent and audience.
2. Confirm distribution and acknowledgement expectations.
3. Track follow-up when a policy changes or is not acknowledged.

## What To Watch

- policy updates without a corresponding acknowledgement plan
- operational rules living only in policy prose with no execution path
- old policy versions still being treated as active
