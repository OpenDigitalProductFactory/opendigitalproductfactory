## Workflow

1. Start with the core portal presentation and live URL settings.
2. Move to business context when the identity, promise, or customer-facing framing is wrong.
3. Use operations settings for hours, availability, and operating constraints that affect real customer interactions.

## What To Watch

- storefront presentation diverging from the actual business context
- hours or operating rules changed without checking customer promises
- route owners editing settings without verifying public impact
