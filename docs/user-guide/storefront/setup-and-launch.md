## Workflow

1. Complete the guided storefront setup in order.
2. Verify the business context, catalog, and operational settings before launch.
3. Check the public route after launch-related changes.

## What To Watch

- partial setup being treated as launch-ready
- archetype or flow choices that do not match the actual business model
- launch decisions being made without public-path verification
