## Overview

Inventory is the operational view of systems, services, infrastructure, and managed items known to the platform. It combines discovered infrastructure with curated records so teams can understand what exists, who owns it, and how it supports delivery.

## Key Concepts

- **Managed items** — The records for infrastructure, services, assets, or configuration items tracked by the platform.
- **Discovery evidence** — Data gathered by discovery jobs, scouts, integrations, or manual review.
- **Ownership** — The team, customer, portfolio, product, or service accountable for the item.
- **Lifecycle state** — Whether the item is active, needs review, has drifted, or should be retired.

## What You Can Do

- Review discovered infrastructure and managed items
- Identify records that need lifecycle review
- Connect items to portfolios, products, customers, or operational services
- Use inventory evidence during incident, compliance, and architecture work
- Spot duplicate, stale, or unowned items that need cleanup
