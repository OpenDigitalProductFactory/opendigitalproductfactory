## Overview

Complaints is the intake and resolution workspace for issues that need formal tracking. Use it when feedback has moved beyond a casual message and needs ownership, status, evidence, and a clear resolution path.

## Key Concepts

- **Complaint records** — The tracked issue, including source, status, priority, owner, and resolution notes.
- **Triage** — The first pass that decides severity, next owner, and whether the complaint links to a customer, order, product, or operational gap.
- **Resolution evidence** — Notes, actions, and attachments that show what changed and why the complaint can be closed.
- **Follow-up** — Any action required after resolution, including customer communication or backlog work.

## What You Can Do

- Review open complaints and understand their status
- Triage incoming complaints by priority and owner
- Link a complaint to the affected customer or operational area
- Track resolution evidence before closing the record
- Convert recurring complaint patterns into backlog items when the product or process needs to change
