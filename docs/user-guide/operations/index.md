## Overview

Operations is the delivery backlog for the platform. It tracks the work items, epics, and priorities that make up your team's delivery commitments. It is designed for transparency — blockers are visible, WIP limits are enforced, and progress is always up to date.

## Key Concepts

- **Backlog Items** — Individual units of work with a status, priority, epic, owner, and optional due date. Items move through statuses: To Do, In Progress, Blocked, Done.
- **Epics** — Groups of related backlog items that together deliver a meaningful outcome. Epics have their own progress tracking based on the status of their items.
- **Priority** — Items are ranked by priority to make the most important work visible. Priorities can be adjusted as circumstances change.
- **WIP Limits** — Constraints on how many items can be In Progress at once. Helps teams avoid overload and finish work before starting new items.
- **Blockers** — Items flagged as blocked surface prominently so they can be unblocked quickly. Blockers are a key signal in daily operations.

## What You Can Do

- View the full delivery backlog and filter by epic, status, priority, or owner
- Create and update backlog items, assign owners, and set due dates
- Group items into epics and track epic-level progress
- Identify and resolve blockers before they stall delivery
- Adjust priorities as delivery context changes
